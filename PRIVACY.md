# PopTranslate — Privacy Policy

PopTranslate does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

When you select text on a webpage, that text is sent directly to the MyMemory translation API (mymemory.translated.net) solely to retrieve a translation. This is a direct browser-to-API request. PopTranslate itself does not see, store, log, or transmit your selected text to any server controlled by the developer.

Your language preferences (source and target language) are saved locally using chrome.storage.sync, which syncs across your own Chrome profile only. This data never leaves your Google account.

No analytics, no tracking, no advertising, no third-party data sharing.
